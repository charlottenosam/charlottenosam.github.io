About Me

I am a NASA Hubble and CfA Fellow at the Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics. I did my PhD with Prof. Tommaso Treu at UCLA and UCSB. I am interested in galaxy evolution at very high redshifts (z > 8), and the relationship between cosmic reionization and galaxy evolution. See my papers on ADS
I was an undergraduate at Merton College, University of Oxford.
